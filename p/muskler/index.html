Firehovedede knæstrækker

Quadriceps Femoris

Den firehovede knæstrækker består af fire muskler. Yderst ligger vastus lateralis og inderst ligger vastus medialis. Inders lever vastus intermedius et ret ubemærket liv, fordi rectus femoris er forrest på låret og dækker over musklen.

Funktioner for Firehovedede knæstrækker

  • Ekstension i knæet

    Quadriceps har fire hoveder, som alle er med til at strække knæet.

  • Fleksion i hoften

    Det lige hoved (rectus femoris) er med til at lave en mindre fleksion i hoften.

Flexor Carpi Ulnaris

Flexor Carpi Ulnaris

  • Udspring

    The flexor carpi ulnaris has two heads; a humeral head and ulnar head. The humeral head originates from the medial epicondyle of the humerus via the common flexor tendon. The ulnar head originates from the medial margin of the olecranon of the ulnar and the upper two-thirds of the dorsal border of the ulnar by an aponeurosis. Between the two heads passes the ulnar nerve and ulnar artery.

  • Hæfte

    The flexor carpi ulnaris inserts onto the pisiform, hook of the hamate (via the pisohamate ligament) and the anterior surface of the base of the fifth metacarpal (via the pisometacarpal ligament).

Funktioner for Ydre skrå mavemuskel

  • Laterally flexes the spine.

    This means that the external oblique muscle bends the spine to the side (i.e. it moves the ribcage downward to the side towards the pelvis).

  • Rotates the spine.

    This means that the external oblique muscle turns the spine to the side (i.e. it twists the torso).

  • Compresses the abdominal cavity.

    This means that the external oblique muscle constricts the organs of the abdominal cavity and can increase intra-abdominal pressure (i.e. such as during a valsalva maneuver).

  • Medially rotates the upper arm.

    This means that the pectoralis major muscle rotates the upper arm inward around the axis of the bone (i.e. it rotates the upper arm toward the vertical midline of the body).
